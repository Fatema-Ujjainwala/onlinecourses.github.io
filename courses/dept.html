VARIOUS DOMAIN COURSES OFFERED BY THE DEPARTMENT


Since the college has achieved Autonomy under Mumbai University, each department of our college offers a variety of courses to the students.
Each department includes 2 domains of their background, each domain has semester-wise courses. The students are given a choice to opt for a particular domain they want to achieve specialization in. After completion of each course the college offers you 3 credits. This credits are included in the marksheet. These credits are summed up in the end of the degree.

Guidelines before Selecting a Domain


1. Students should opt for a single domain for specialization.
2. Students can opt for Domains from any department.
3. Only after scoring 60% of marks in each course, the students will receive the 3 credits.
4. In the 8th Semester, the students have to submit a mini project based on their domain which will grab you 2 credits.
5. To get the degree of specialization, students should achieve a total of 20 credits.
NOTE: Students can give n number of attempts during the course of engineering.
